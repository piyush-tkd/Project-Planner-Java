FINDING #1: TAB HUB DUPLICATION
Problem: Users can access the same page from EITHER:
  a) Directly via sidebar item
  b) Via hub -> tab
This creates navigation confusion and maintenance burden.

Affected Items:
  - "Status Updates" can be reached from Jira section sidebar OR Portfolio Health hub
  - "Project Health" can be reached from Portfolio section sidebar OR Portfolio Health hub
  - "DORA Metrics" can be reached from Delivery section sidebar OR Engineering Hub
  - "Engineering Intelligence" can be reached from Delivery section sidebar OR Engineering Hub
  - "Delivery Predictability" can be reached from Delivery section sidebar OR Engineering Hub
  - "Resource Intelligence" can be reached from People section sidebar OR Performance Hub
  - "Resource Bookings" can be reached from People section sidebar OR Resources Hub
  - "Overrides" can be reached from People section sidebar OR Resources Hub OR Capacity Hub

FINDING #2: ORPHANED STANDALONE PAGES
Problem: Some pages are accessible ONLY from the sidebar but are also components within hubs.
- "Advanced Timeline" is sidebar item but overlaps with Portfolio Timeline hub
- "Workload Chart" is sidebar item but concept overlaps with Capacity Hub
- "Scenario Planning" page exists but also has scenario tools hub

FINDING #3: SETTINGS PAGES NOT IN SIDEBAR
Problem: 29 settings pages exist but Settings section shows only 12 items in sidebar + SettingsHubPage.tsx
- SettingsHubPage is a hub with 12 category cards (not tabs)
- But navRegistry lists 23 settings items
- Unclear which settings pages are actually shown in sidebar vs hub

FINDING #4: NAMING CONFLICTS
Problems:
  - 5+ "Timeline" pages with different purposes
  - 5+ "Dashboard" pages with different purposes  
  - 5+ "Health" pages with overlapping concepts
  - 5+ "Resource" pages scattered across sections
  - 3+ "Capacity" pages with unclear separation

FINDING #5: INCONSISTENT NAVIGATION PATTERNS
- Some hubs use tabs (PortfolioHealthTabs, ResourcesTabs, etc.)
- Settings uses a card-grid layout (SettingsHubPage) instead of tabs
- Some standalone pages also exist as tabs
- No consistent mental model for users

FINDING #6: MISSING/ORPHANED PAGES
